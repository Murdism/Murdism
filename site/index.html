Research

What I Work On

My research focuses on perception and motion prediction for autonomous systems, with a focus on making these systems reliable under the conditions where they currently break. I am interested in calibrated 3D perception under multi-modal sensor fusion, multi-agent trajectory prediction, and how reliability signals from perception can support safer downstream decisions. Complementary interests include large-scale dataset development and robust generalization across different environments and domains.
